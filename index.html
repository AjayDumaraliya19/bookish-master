    Martin Jenny

    Style: Art, Fiction

    Jenny Martin is an author, librarian, and Beatle-maniac. Her first novel, Tracked, is available now, from Dial, an imprint of Penguin Random-House. She lives in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, with her husband and son, where she hoards books and writes fiction, and hangs out with people who like those things, too. And yes, she’s still on a quest for the perfect pancake.
